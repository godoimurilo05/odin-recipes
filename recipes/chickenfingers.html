Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 2 eggs
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Optional: herbs or spices of your choice (such as paprika, garlic powder)
  • Cooking oil, for frying (if frying)

Steps

  1. Prepare Chicken: Cut boneless, skinless chicken breasts into strips.
  2. Coat with Breadcrumbs: In one bowl, mix breadcrumbs with salt, pepper, and optional herbs or spices. In another bowl, beat eggs.
  3. Dip and Coat: Dip each chicken strip first in beaten eggs, then coat evenly with seasoned breadcrumbs.
  4. Cook: Heat cooking oil in a skillet over medium heat. Fry chicken strips in batches until golden brown and cooked through, about 3-4 minutes per side. Alternatively, bake on a lightly greased baking sheet at 400°F (200°C) for 20-25 minutes, flipping halfway through.
  5. Serve: Drain on paper towels if fried, and serve hot with your favorite dipping sauces. These homemade chicken fingers are sure to be a hit, offering a deliciously crispy exterior and tender, juicy chicken inside that everyone will love.
