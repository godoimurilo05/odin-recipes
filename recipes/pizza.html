Pepperoni pizza

Description

Savor the simple pleasure of homemade Pepperoni Pizza with our easy recipe. Start by preparing a classic pizza dough, letting it rise until doubled. Roll it out thinly and spread with a flavorful tomato sauce made from ripe tomatoes, garlic, and oregano. Layer on slices of spicy pepperoni and a generous amount of shredded mozzarella cheese. Bake until the crust is golden and crisp, and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.

Serve this Pepperoni Pizza hot and fresh, where each slice offers a delightful blend of tangy tomato sauce, gooey cheese, and savory pepperoni. Perfect for a casual dinner or a cozy night in, this homemade pizza captures the comforting essence of a classic favorite.

Ingredients

  • 1 batch of pizza dough (homemade or store-bought)
  • 1 cup tomato sauce (homemade or store-bought)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 20-25 slices of pepperoni
  • Freshly grated Parmesan cheese (optional)
  • Fresh basil leaves, torn (optional)
  • Olive oil, for drizzling

Steps

  1. Prepare Dough: Follow your pizza dough recipe, letting it rise until doubled.
  2. Preheat Oven: Set to highest temperature; place pizza stone or baking sheet inside.
  3. Make Sauce: Mix tomato sauce with garlic and oregano.
  4. Roll Out Dough: Shape into a 12-14 inch round, place on parchment paper.
  5. Assemble Pizza: Spread sauce on dough, top with mozzarella, arrange pepperoni.
  6. Bake: Transfer pizza to preheated stone or sheet, bake for 10-12 mins until golden and bubbly.
  7. Serve: Optional: sprinkle with Parmesan and basil before slicing and enjoying hot.
